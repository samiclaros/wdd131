Essential Site Plan

Sitename

Essential Clothes

Domain

essential-clothes.bo

Purpose

Our purpose is to help future missionaries with the things they will need for the mission, the most essential and we ensure quality in our products.

Scenarios

1. I need things that will last me 2 years...

We can assure that our products last 2 years, otherwise we offer a new garment

2. Will the clothes be expensive?

It will be available to anyone, obviously we have to earn something from this, but our purpose is to help and not just profit.

3. Will it be of good quality?

If it is, we have made these clothes and we have tested them with relatives who went on the mission and if they lasted at least 24 months
